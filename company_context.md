Digital Agents Interactive (DAI) is an India-based immersive technology company specializing in **Extended Reality (XR)**, **Artificial Intelligence (AI)**, **Digital Twins**, **Simulators**, **Spatial Apps**, and **Enterprise Metaverses**. The company serves clients across enterprise, defence, education, healthcare, manufacturing, and consumer sectors globally.

DAI operates at the intersection of creativity and cutting-edge technology, building solutions that range from VR training simulators for defence organizations to immersive e-learning tools for global FMCG brands. Their work spans both consumer-facing experiences and enterprise-grade operational platforms.

## Technology Solutions (Products)

### Digital Twin
Real-time, high-fidelity virtual replicas of physical assets — enabling predictive maintenance, operational monitoring, and scenario simulation across industries.

### Immersive 360° Virtual Tours
Photorealistic virtual walkthroughs used for real estate, industrial facilities, hospitals, and educational campuses.

### AI-Powered Defect Identification
Computer vision systems integrated with production lines for automated, real-time defect detection and quality assurance.

### Remote AR Assistance
AR-enabled remote collaboration tools allowing experts to guide field technicians through complex tasks via live video and annotation overlays.

### 3D Product Experiences
Configurable, interactive 3D product visualizations for e-commerce, automotive showrooms, and retail environments.

### Interactive Technical Manuals (iTMs)
Digitized, AR/VR-overlaid manuals that replace paper-based technical documentation with step-by-step interactive guidance.

### Injection Simulators
Haptic-feedback and VR-based medical training simulators used to train healthcare professionals in injection procedures without risk to patients.

## Notable Projects & Case Studies

### 1. 24x7 Mobile Learning Tool — Global FMCG Leader
**Industry:** Retail & FMCG | **Type:** Mobile Learning
Developed a continuous mobile learning platform for the future workforce of a global FMCG leader. The solution equipped employees with on-demand, bite-sized training modules accessible anytime, resulting in improved productivity and skill development at scale.

---

### 2. Game-Based Sales Training — Leading Beverage Company (Coca-Cola)
**Industry:** Beverage | **Type:** 3D / Game-Based Training
Revolutionized sales training for a leading beverage company using a game-based approach. The gamified training program dramatically improved learning outcomes and employee engagement compared to traditional training formats.

> *"Working with Digital Agents has been a game-changer for us. Their expertise in XR technology allowed us to create immersive training modules that are engaging, interactive, and highly effective."*
> — **Alok Saxena, AVP, Information Technology, Coca-Cola**

---

### 3. VisTaS — Visualization & Immersive Simulation of Tactical Scenarios
**Industry:** Defence | **Type:** VR & AI
Delivered an advanced visualization and immersive simulation platform (VisTaS) enabling military personnel to train for complex tactical scenarios. The platform supports enhanced situational awareness, decision-making under pressure, and collaborative mission planning.

---

### 4. Simulation-Based Digital Twin VR Platform
**Industry:** Engineering / Technology | **Type:** Digital Twin + VR
Built a simulation-based Digital Twin VR platform for an engineering client (Siemens). The platform was delivered ahead of schedule and brought significant operational value, enabling teams to simulate, test, and optimize physical systems in a fully virtual environment.

> *"The Digital Agents Interactive team showcased exceptional professionalism and dedication, consistently delivering high-quality results ahead of schedule."*
> — **Nitin Jain, Programme Head – EE Industry and Data, Digi-Twin and EE Cooling**

---

### 5. Alternate Reality Tools for Submersible Acoustic Characterization — NPOL
**Industry:** Defence / Naval Research | **Type:** AR/VR
Successfully supplied, installed, and commissioned Alternate Reality Tools for Simulated Survey and Digital Pedagogy for Experimentation aboard a submersible platform for acoustic characterization for the Naval Physical and Oceanographic Laboratory (NPOL), Government of India. The project was completed and accepted in March 2023.

> *"Their commendable work and timely execution have significantly contributed to our project objectives."*
> — **Shan Victor Pereira, Project Director, SPACE PROJECT, NPOL**

---

### 6. vFabLab — Virtual Nano-Fabrication Laboratory
**Institution:** Purdue University (USA) | **Type:** VR Education
Built and maintained a Virtual Laboratory for Nano-fabrication Operations Training (vFabLab) for Purdue University over a seven-year partnership. The platform empowers students across the globe to gain practical nanotechnology skills in a safe, accessible virtual environment — bridging the gap between theory and hands-on lab experience.

> *"Your technical expertise and dedication have been vital in developing our Virtual Laboratory for Nano-fabrication Operations Training. The innovative solutions provided have empowered students worldwide."*
> — **Dr. Muhammad Mustafa Hussain, Purdue University**

---

### 7. Metaverse Events Platform — Vibo
**Industry:** Events & Entertainment | **Type:** Metaverse / SaaS Platform
Transformed virtual event management with Vibo, a no-code metaverse platform offering immersive and interactive virtual experiences. Deployed for events, conferences, product launches, and large-scale corporate gatherings globally.
